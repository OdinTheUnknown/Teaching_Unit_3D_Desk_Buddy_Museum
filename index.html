To access the virtual museum, click on the window below. Once it has finished loading, left-click to lock your mouse to the window, or click the full-screen button (bottom-right corner) to enlarge the windows. You can press ESC at any time to release your mouse/screen from the window.

CONTROLS: WASD to move, move mouse to look around, left-click while looking at information buttons to toggle audio on/off.

Please wait patiently for the window to load; it may take up to three minutes depending on your internet connection speed. If any problems occur, try refreshing the page. If problems continue to persist, a downloadable version of the museum can be found in the "Additional Resources" section below.
